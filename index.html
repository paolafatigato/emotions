Compare!
Use comparatives & superlatives to talk about your emotions 🆚🏆
SHORT ADJECTIVES (-er)
happy → happier
sad → sadder
scared → more scared
"I feel happier when I eat pizza than when I eat salad."
LONG ADJECTIVES (more...)
excited → more excited
nervous → more nervous
disgusted → more disgusted
"I feel more nervous before a test than before lunch."
SHORT ADJECTIVES (the -est)
happy → the happiest
sad → the saddest
funny → the funniest
"This is the happiest I have ever felt!"
LONG ADJECTIVES (the most...)
excited → the most excited
disgusted → the most disgusted
"This is the most terrifying thing I can imagine."
